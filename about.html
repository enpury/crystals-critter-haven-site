About Crystal's Critter Haven

Big hearts for every kind of critter.

Crystal's Critter Haven is a volunteer operated nonprofit in Mesa, Arizona, providing rescue, rehabilitation, release, adoption, and lifelong sanctuary for wild and domestic animals. The Haven specializes in medical cases, neonatal care, and animals with complex or ongoing needs.

A safe place for every kind of animal ♡

Our mission

Care for every path forward.

Our mission is to rescue and rehabilitate wild and domestic animals, return those who can safely go home, prepare others for loving adoptive families, and provide lifelong sanctuary to those who cannot return or be adopted.

Through public education, the Haven also helps people make safer and kinder decisions when wildlife and animals need help.

Meet Crystal

For Crystal, every life is worth fighting for.

Born and raised in Mesa, Arizona, Crystal cannot remember a life without animals. She grew up raising them, caring for them, and bottle feeding fragile babies who depended on her for every meal, every comfort, and every chance to grow stronger.

Crystal is a Certified Veterinary Technician, a wildlife rehabilitator licensed by both the state and federal government, and the founder of Crystal's Critter Haven. Her clinical knowledge and lifelong hands on experience guide the individualized care each animal receives.

The Haven's earliest work centered on animals placed through law enforcement and the Department of Agriculture. Crystal also supported local veterinary teams by accepting surrendered animals that needed continued medical or specialized care.

“This is my heart, my soul, and my passion.”Crystal, Founder of Crystal's Critter Haven

Community powered care

Help make more second chances possible.

Crystal's Critter Haven is operated entirely by volunteers and supported primarily by individual donors and community partners. Every contribution helps provide food, medical supplies, safe habitats, and specialized care.

A dedicated facility would create more room to heal, more ways to volunteer, and more opportunities for the community to learn.

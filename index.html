About Crystal's Critter Haven

A safe place for every kind of animal.

Based in Mesa, Arizona, Crystal's Critter Haven provides compassionate rescue, rehabilitation, release, adoption, and lifelong sanctuary for wild, domestic, neonatal, and special needs animals. Founded by Crystal in 2018, the Haven gives every animal individualized care and the best possible chance to heal and thrive.

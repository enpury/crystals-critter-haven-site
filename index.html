Animal emergency?

Found a sick, injured, or orphaned animal?

  • Keep the animal warm, safe, and secure in a ventilated box or pet carrier.
  • Do not give food or water.
  • Text a picture of the animal and your nearest major crossroads to 480-220-6930.
  • Get the animal into care as soon as possible. Be prepared to transport it unless other arrangements are made.

These instructions are for non-dangerous wildlife. Do not approach or handle an animal that could injure you.
